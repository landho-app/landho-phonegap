Money

Philippine peso (PHP) of 100 centavos

Communications

International Dialing Code for the Philippines is +63.

A prepaid Philippines SIM card with an international cell phone is most convenient and economical. All incoming calls in the Philippines are FREE, regardless of where they originate. The Philippines SIM card provides service across the Philippines through domestic roaming. Pay the local rate for local calls and use a cellular phone in the Philippines.

Events

All Souls Regatta
http://www.pgyc.org/home.php
The annual All Souls Regatta was organized first in 2004 with two objectives: to provide the Philippine sailing community with a fun regatta at the start of the competitive sailing season; and, to kick-start the holiday season in Puerto Galera a month earlier than usual. 30 October to 1 November 2015
Festival Derawan 2013
http://sailsamal.com/blog/post/2012/12/28/Festival-Derawan-Yacht-rally-2013-from-Davao-to-Derawan-Indonesia.aspx
Following the success of Sail Morotai 2012 rally from Davao, Philippines to the Derawan Islands Indonesia, the next rally for 2013 is to be called the FESTIVAL DERAWAN 2013 because the activities in Derawan will be divided into 2 parts: June 2013 - Diving competition, September 2013 - yacht rally (from Malaysia and from Davao).
Philippine Hobie Challenge
http://www.hobiechallenge.ph/
The Philippine Hobie Challenge takes sailors on a scenic route through a different part of the Philippines each year; in 2016, the Challenge will take competitors and cruising yachts from Mactan Island (Cebu) to Panglao, Bohol. Cruising yachts follow the challengers in a Rally. Now in its 16th year, the Philippine Hobie Challenge is one of the longest running international sailing events in the country. Starts 20th February 2016.
Sail BIMP-EAGA
Exploring Equator-Asia
http://www.bruneibay.net/SailBIMP-EAGA/index.html
Annual programme of four yacht cruises designed to highlight the cultural attractions and amazing biodiversity of Brunei, Indonesia, Malaysia and the Philippines. Started 2009. Detailed rally info. on website.

Emergency services - Dial 117
Police - Dial 168
